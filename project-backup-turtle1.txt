2. CORE MODES
============================================================

2.1 Local 2 Players
- Mode value: local
- Dung luat chess.js chuan.
- KHONG cho an Vua.
- KHONG dung capture king logic.

2.2 Player vs Bot
- Mode value: bot
- Dung chess.js chuan.
- Player chon mau w/b.
- Bot di sau delay ~300ms khi toi luot bot.
- Bot khong di neu game over.
- Premove tat trong bot mode de tranh nhieu.
- Undo/Redo trong bot mode:
  - Undo 1 lan lui 1 cap nuoc neu co: bot move + player move.
  - Neu player cam den va moi co nuoc bot mo dau, undo lui 1 nuoc bot.
  - Redo 1 lan replay lai ca cap nuoc neu co.
  - Redo khong schedule bot di them nua.

Bot standard difficulties:
- Random: chooseRandomMove()
- Greedy: chooseGreedyMove()
- Minimax: getBestMove(game), minimax depth 2 ply.

3. TURTLE 1 LOGIC
============================================================

3.1 Turtle 1 - So sinh
Muc tieu:
- Hoc cach di quan.
- Hoc an quan.
- Hoc gia tri quan.

Bot:
- De, nhung khong qua ngo ngan.
- Luon an Vua neu co.
- Uu tien an quan mien phi/gia tri cao.
- Tranh tu lam lo Vua.
- Tranh tu dua Hau vao o bi an ngay qua ro.
- Neu khong co tactic, uu tien phat trien Ma/Tuong don gian.

3.2 Turtle 1 - Thi len lop
Muc tieu:
- Kiem tra hoc sinh da nam kien thuc Turtle 1 chua.
- Gia tri quan.
- An hon quan.
- Nhin quan khong duoc bao ve.
- Tranh o khong an toan.
- Phong thu co ban.
- Bao ve Vua.

Bot:
- Khong phai engine manh.
- Cam giac khoang beginner/200-400 Elo.
- Khong bo lo an Vua.
- Khong bo lo an Hau/Xe mien phi lon.
- Khong tu thi Hau/Xe vo ly neu co lua chon an toan.
- Neu Vua dang nguy hiem, uu tien cuu Vua.
- Co the chon trong top candidates de van yeu co kiem soat.

3.3 Luat an Vua
- chess.js chuan khong cho an Vua.
- Turtle 1 dung getTurtlePseudoMovesFrom() va getAllTurtlePseudoMoves().
- isKingCaptureMove(from, to) chi return true trong isTurtle1Mode().
- makeTurtleMove() co guard: neu khong phai Turtle 1 thi return false.
- endByKingCapture() co guard: neu khong phai Turtle 1 thi return.

3.4 Xac dinh thang/thua
- winnerColor la mau quan vua an duoc Vua.
- Neu winnerColor === playerColor:
  - VI: "Tuyet voi! Con da quan sat tot va an duoc Vua roi 🎉"
  - EN: "Great job! You observed well and captured the King 🎉"
- Neu bot an Vua nguoi choi:
  - VI: "Oi mat Vua roi :< Lan sau nho quan sat truoc khi di nha!"
  - EN: "Oh no, the King was captured :< Remember to observe before moving!"
- Neutral local capture king text ton tai trong translations nhung khong duoc dung trong Local 2 Players chuan.

3.5 Undo sau capture king
- Turtle 1 undo dung turtleMoveHistory.
- Neu undo sau khi mat/an Vua:
  - turtleGameOver = false
  - turtleWinner = null
  - game load ve fenBefore cua move da undo.
  - Game tiep tuc duoc.
- Undo counter tang 1 moi lan undo thanh cong trong Turtle 1, du undo lui 1 hoac 2 half-moves.

4.3 Scoring constants
- KING_CAPTURE_SCORE = 10000
- KING_DANGER_PENALTY = 5000
- QUEEN_BLUNDER_PENALTY = 900
- ROOK_BLUNDER_PENALTY = 420
- FREE_CAPTURE_BONUS = 130
- PROFITABLE_TRADE_BONUS = 90
- DEVELOPMENT_BONUS = 30
- REPEATED_PIECE_PENALTY = 35
- EARLY_ROOK_MOVE_PENALTY = 55
- EARLY_QUEEN_MOVE_PENALTY = 45
- DEBUG_TURTLE_BOT = false

4.4 Cach evaluate Turtle 1 move
scoreTurtle1PromotionTestMove(move) tinh diem dua tren:
- An Vua: diem rat lon, return ngay.
- Move lam lo Vua bi an: phat KING_DANGER_PENALTY.
- Neu Vua dang bi de doa va move xu ly duoc: cong diem lon.
- An quan mien phi: FREE_CAPTURE_BONUS + gia tri quan.
- Trao doi co loi: PROFITABLE_TRADE_BONUS + chenhlech value.
- An Hau/Xe mien phi: bonus rat lon de khong bo lo.
- Move bi an lai boi quan re hon/bang diem: phat manh.
- Di Hau khong capture qua som: EARLY_QUEEN_MOVE_PENALTY.
- Di Xe qua lai/back rank khong can thiet: EARLY_ROOK_MOVE_PENALTY.
- Di cung quan lien tiep/reverse move: REPEATED_PIECE_PENALTY.
- Phat trien Ma/Tuong tu hang nha: DEVELOPMENT_BONUS.
- Trung tam: centerBonus().

4.5 Bao ve Vua
- isKingUnderBasicThreat(color): Vua dang bi tan cong bang pseudo moves.
- doesMoveExposeKingToCapture(move, color): gia lap move roi xem Vua co bi an ngay khong.
- findKingDefenseMoves(color): loc moves khong lam Vua bi an.
- Turtle 1 - Thi len lop:
  - Neu Vua dang bi de doa va co defense moves, chi xet defense moves.
  - Uu tien an quan dang de doa, chay Vua an toan, hoac che/bao ve neu scoring thay tot.

4.6 Tranh mat Hau/Xe
- moveLooksSafe(move): xem quan vua di co bi an ngay khong.
- findHighValuePiecesUnderAttack(color): tim Hau/Xe/Vua dang bi tan cong.
- Phat manh neu move bo qua Hau dang bi de doa.
- Phat rat manh neu Hau/Xe di vao o khong an toan.

4.7 Random co kiem soat
- Turtle 1 - Thi len lop co the chon trong top 2 candidates neu diem gan nhau.
- Khong random khi:
  - An Vua.
  - An Hau/Xe mien phi diem lon.
  - Cuu Vua.
  - Tranh mat Hau/Xe lon.

- getTurtlePseudoMovesFrom(from): generator pseudo moves cho Turtle 1, cho phep an Vua.

- isKingCaptureMove(from, to): phai return false neu !isTurtle1Mode().
- makeTurtleMove(moveData, options): phai return false neu !isTurtle1Mode().

- clearLearningState(): reset turtleGameOver, turtleWinner, turtleMoveHistory, turtleRedoStack, undoCount, lastBotMove.

Turtle bots:
- chooseTurtle1NewbornBotMove(): bot de nhung khong qua ngo.

- chooseTurtle1StandardBotMove(): wrapper goi chooseTurtle1PromotionTestBotMove(), giu compatibility.
- DEBUG_TURTLE_BOT: false mac dinh, bat true de log candidates.

Undo/Redo:
- undoMove(): Local standard 1 half-move; bot/Turtle theo cap.
- redoMove(): Local standard 1 half-move; bot/Turtle theo cap.
- updateLastMoveFromHistory(): dung cho standard mode.

UI/render:
