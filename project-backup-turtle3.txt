7A. Opening Rule Engine
- Turtle 3 co Opening Rule Engine rieng.
- Day theo "6 nguyen tac vang":
  1. Phat trien Ma/Tuong.
  2. Khong di 1 quan qua nhieu lan.
  3. Khong dua Hau ra som neu khong an toan va khong co muc dich ro.
  4. Huong vao trung tam.
  5. Khong can tro phat trien.
  6. Khong spam chot phu.

Helper chinh:
- evaluateTurtle3OpeningRules(...)
- getTurtle3OpeningProfile(...)
- doesMoveDevelopMinor(...)
- doesMoveDevelopQueenSafely(...)
- doesMoveCastle(...)
- doesMoveActivateRook(...)
- doesMoveControlCenter(...)
- doesMoveBlockDevelopment(...)
- isUnnecessaryPawnMove(...)
- isRepeatedPieceMoveAllowed(...)

Penalties/bonuses opening dang co:
- phat trien quan nhe
- control center
- castling
- rook activation
- early queen penalty
- repeated piece penalty
- knight rim penalty
- block development penalty
- extra pawn move penalty
- premature pawn break penalty
- backward retreat / return home / undevelopment / wander penalties

7B. Phase Detection

7D. Opening Book
- Opening book thu gon, khong da dang qua.
- Uu tien Italian / basic e4-e5 structures.
- Co line Ruy Lopez de tranh ...Nb8 vo ly.

Mau opening:
- Trang:
  - e4
  - Nf3
  - Bc4
  - O-O
  - d3 / c3 / Re1
- Den:
  - ...e5
  - ...Nc6
  - ...Bc5 hoac ...Nf6
  - ...a6 / ...Nf6 trong Ruy Lopez

Book phai obey rule engine:
- neu vi pham opening rules nang thi bi loai
- neu co danger thi bo qua book

7E. Tactical System
- Tactical system da duoc tune lai.
- Trong evaluateTurtle3Move() hien co:
  - priority material
  - safe capture bonus
  - attacker/defender profile
  - anti panic
  - anti return-home vo ly

Uu tien material:
- an Hau/Xe free hoac trade loi phai duoc uu tien hon viec chay minor piece
- constants moi:
  - TURTLE3_WIN_QUEEN_BONUS
  - TURTLE3_WIN_ROOK_BONUS
  - TURTLE3_WIN_MINOR_BONUS
  - TURTLE3_SAFE_CAPTURE_BONUS

Attacker/defender:
- getThreatProfile(...) tra ve:
  - attackersCount
  - defendersCount
  - weakestAttackerValue
  - weakestDefenderValue
  - pieceValue
  - isHanging
  - isUnderDefended
  - isBadTrade
  - isActuallyInDanger

Anti panic:
- neu bi tan cong nhung defenders du va trade khong lo, bot khong panic
- neu attackers > defenders hoac treo hoac trade xau, bot phai phan ung

Anti return-home:
- phat nang ...Nb8 / ...Ng8 / bishop ve nha vo ly
- chi cho phep lui ve nha khi co emergency ro rang

7F. Endgame Design
- Endgame ngang quan:
  - yeu co chu dich
  - khong biet deep opposition
  - co the bo lo ke hoach thang
  - khong random lo lieu
- Endgame hon chat ro:
  - biet doi quan co ban
  - biet day chot an toan hon
  - biet dua Vua hoat dong hon
  - khong duoc quang loi the mot cach vo ly

Helper lien quan:
